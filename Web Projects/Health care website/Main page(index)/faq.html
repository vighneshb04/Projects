FAQs

What services does Raphael Healthcare provide?

Raphael Healthcare provides a range of essential healthcare services tailored for university hostels, including real-time ambulance tracking, secure patient information sharing, isolation alerts for managing infectious diseases, pharmacy delivery services, and continuous monitoring of medical implants.

How does ambulance tracking work on your platform?

Our platform uses GPS technology to track ambulances in real-time. This ensures that during emergencies, we can dispatch the nearest available ambulance to provide timely medical assistance to those in need within university hostels.

Is patient information sharing secure?

Yes, we prioritize the security and confidentiality of patient information. Our platform employs robust encryption and access control measures to ensure that only authorized healthcare providers and relevant authorities can access and share patient information as needed for effective medical care.

How does real-time monitoring of implantable devices benefit residents?

Real-time monitoring allows for continuous tracking of medical implants, providing immediate alerts and updates in case of anomalies. This proactive approach ensures timely intervention and enhances the overall safety and health management of residents with medical implants.

How does pharmacy delivery work through your service?

We collaborate with trusted pharmacies to offer convenient medication delivery directly to your doorstep. This service eliminates the need for residents to visit pharmacies, ensuring timely access to medications and promoting adherence to prescribed treatments.
