About Us

Welcome to Rapheal healthcare

Your comprehensive healthcare communication platform tailored for university hostels.We are committed to ensuring the well-being and safety of every student through seamless and efficient healthcare services.

Our Serices include

Ambulance Tracking: Real-time tracking of ambulances to ensure timely medical assistance during emergencies.
Patient Info Sharing: Secure and confidential sharing of patient information between healthcare providers and relevant authorities.
Isolation Alerts: Instant alerts and notifications about isolation requirements to manage infectious diseases and ensure safety.
Pharmacy Delivery: Convenient and prompt delivery of medications from our partnered pharmacies directly to your doorstep.
Real-time Monitoring of Implantable Devices: Continuous monitoring of medical implants to provide real-time health updates and immediate response to any anomalies.
Acessibility for All Impairments: Our platform is designed to be fully accessible, catering to users with visual, auditory, motor, and cognitive impairments

Our mission is to revolutionize healthcare communication in university hostels by providing a user-friendly, accessible, and reliable platform. We aim to bridge the gap between healthcare providers and students, ensuring that medical assistance is always within reach.
Join us in making university hostels a safer and healthier place for everyone. Together, we can ensure that every student has access to the best possible healthcare services.

Our Services

Ambulance Tracking

Our advanced ambulance tracking system utilizes GPS technology to monitor the location of ambulances in real-time. This ensures that emergency medical services can be dispatched quickly and efficiently to those in need, reducing response times and saving lives.

Isolation Alerts

In response to emerging infectious diseases, our system delivers instant alerts regarding isolation protocols and requirements. This helps healthcare facilities and individuals effectively manage outbreaks, reduce transmission risks, and safeguard community health.

Real-Time Monitoring Of Implantable Devices

We are committed to inclusivity and have designed our platform to be fully accessible, accommodating users with diverse needs including visual, auditory, motor, and cognitive impairments.

Patient Info Sharing

Facilitating seamless communication, our platform enables healthcare providers to securely share vital patient information with authorized personnel and relevant authorities. This ensures coordinated care, accurate diagnoses, and timely treatment decisions, all while maintaining strict confidentiality.

Pharmacy Delivery

Partnering with trusted pharmacies, we offer a convenient pharmacy delivery service, ensuring patients receive their medications promptly and directly at their doorstep. This eliminates the need for unnecessary trips to pharmacies, enhances medication adherence, and promotes overall health and well-being.

Acessibility For All Impairments

We are committed to inclusivity and have designed our platform to be fully accessible, accommodating users with diverse needs including visual, auditory, motor, and cognitive impairments. We ensure that everyone can access and benefit from our healthcare services equitably.
